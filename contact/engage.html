Engage

One place to request a consultation, submit an RFP, or join the newsletter.

This page provides a unified funnel to engage with Truzen Consulting. You can request a consultation, share basic RFP details, or subscribe to governance-focused updates using a single, consolidated structure.

Choose how you’d like to engage

Consultation, RFP, or newsletter

Select the option that best matches your current need. The form below adapts to your choice through simple field groupings (front-end only). No back-end integration is implied on this page.

Book a consultation

Share a concise summary of your AI, data, or governance questions and request an initial consultation.

  • AI / data strategy discussions
  • Governance & operating model topics
  • Leadership or board-oriented briefings

Use the form below and select Book consultation as your engagement type.

Submit an RFP / RFI

Provide high-level RFP or RFI context and a non-confidential summary of what you are seeking from an advisory partner.

  • Basic scope and timelines
  • Relevant domains (AI, data, governance)
  • Any public or sharable documentation links

Use the form below and select RFP / RFI as your engagement type.

Join the newsletter

Indicate interest in curated updates on AI strategy, governance, data foundations, and Truzen publications.

  • Occasional governance-focused notes
  • Updates on new PoVs and research
  • No day-to-day marketing emails

Use the form below and select Newsletter as your engagement type.

Guidance

What this funnel is designed to do

Single entry point

Whether you are booking a consultation, sharing basic RFP context, or joining the newsletter, this page is intentionally designed as a single, unified entry point.

Professional and minimal

The form asks only for information that is typically required to respond professionally. It avoids requesting sensitive details or confidential documentation at this stage.

Data handling note

Please do not share confidential, regulated, or highly sensitive information here. If deeper materials or formal RFP documents need to be exchanged, appropriate secure channels can be agreed in subsequent communication.
