Privacy Policy

How Truzen Consulting handles your information.

This policy explains what information may be collected on this website, how it may be used, and the choices available to you. The details below remain intentionally minimal and generic unless additional specifics are provided.

Overview

1. Introduction

Truzen Consulting (“we”, “our”, “the firm”) is a boutique advisory organization. This privacy policy describes how information may be collected, used, or retained when you access this website or submit a contact enquiry. The policy is designed to be clear, minimal, and aligned with typical professional-services practices.

2. Information we may collect

Truzen Consulting collects only the information necessary to operate this website and respond to enquiries. Depending on your interaction, we may collect:

  • Contact form information: name, email address, organization, and message details you voluntarily submit.
  • Basic technical information: browser type, device type, and general usage patterns, typically collected through standard analytics tools.
  • Log data: non-identifying information automatically provided by your browser when you visit this site.

3. How we use your information

Information is used for professional and operational purposes only, including:

  • Responding to your enquiries or consultation requests.
  • Understanding general usage trends to improve website experience.
  • Maintaining security and ensuring proper website functioning.

4. What we do not do

  • No selling of personal data.
  • No sharing with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • No automated decision-making or profiling.
  • No unnecessary data collection beyond what is described.

5. Legal basis for processing (if applicable)

Where data-protection laws such as GDPR apply, Truzen processes information based on legitimate interest (responding to enquiries) or express consent (when you provide information voluntarily through the contact form).

6. Data retention

Truzen retains information only as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected (for example, responding to enquiries), unless a longer period is legally required. Generic website analytics data may be retained for standard operational periods.

7. Cookies and analytics

This site may use basic cookies or analytics tools to understand aggregate website usage. No sensitive or personally intrusive tracking is performed. You may disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

8. Information sharing

Truzen does not share personal information with third parties except when required by law or when necessary to operate essential service providers (e.g., website hosting), who are subject to confidentiality obligations.

9. Security

Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures are used to protect information from unauthorized access or misuse. No online transmission method can be guaranteed fully secure.

10. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, you may submit a request through the contact page.

11. External websites

This website may include links to external sites. Truzen is not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party websites.

12. Updates to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect operational, legal, or regulatory changes. The most recent update date will be shown here.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to request information, you may contact Truzen through the contact page.
