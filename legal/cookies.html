Cookie Notice

How cookies and similar technologies are used on this website.

This notice explains what cookies may be used on the Truzen Consulting website, why they may be used, and how you can manage your preferences.

Overview

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They allow a site to function properly, remember preferences, or understand general usage patterns. Cookies do not typically identify you directly unless you choose to provide information through a form.

2. Types of cookies that may be used

Truzen Consulting aims to use only minimal and essential cookies. Depending on configuration, the site may use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic site operation (navigation, page loading, security). These cannot be disabled.
  • Performance & analytics cookies (optional): Used to understand aggregate website usage, such as page visits and browser types. Data is non-identifying and used only to improve site performance.
  • Preference cookies (optional): Remember simple settings such as language or region if implemented.

3. What Truzen does not use cookies for

  • No targeted advertising or marketing tracking.
  • No profiling, behavioural analytics, or automated decisions.
  • No sale or sharing of cookie data with third parties.

4. Cookie consent and choices

When optional cookies are in use, you may:

  • Accept all optional cookies.
  • Reject optional cookies and allow only essential cookies.
  • Change your preferences at any time through browser settings.

If no optional cookies are active, this site may not display a cookie banner, as essential cookies alone do not require consent in many jurisdictions.

5. How to manage or disable cookies

You can manage or disable cookies entirely through your browser. Common settings include:

  • Blocking all cookies
  • Deleting specific cookies
  • Allowing cookies only from trusted sites
  • Notifying you when a site attempts to set a cookie

Disabling essential cookies may cause portions of the website to function incorrectly or become inaccessible.

6. Analytics tools (if enabled)

If the website uses analytics, they track only aggregated, non-identifying information such as page views or browser type. No personal profiles are created.

7. Updates to this notice

This Cookie Notice may be updated to reflect changes in technology, regulations, or website configuration. The most recent update date will be recorded here.

8. Contact

Questions regarding this Cookie Notice may be submitted via the contact page.
