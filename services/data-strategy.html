Data Strategy

Data foundations designed for scalable, governed AI.

We help organizations design data strategies that are realistic, governance-aware, and tightly aligned with AI and analytics ambitions—so that data becomes an enabler of AI, not a persistent constraint, across your AI Maturity Model and transformation efforts.

Leadership questions

What we help leadership teams solve

Data Strategy engagements focus on connecting AI ambition with the reality of your data, platforms, and governance structures. We work with data, technology, and business leaders to prioritize where to invest and how to structure data capabilities over time, so that data work remains tightly coupled to AI and analytics outcomes.

What data do we actually need for AI?

Clarify which domains, sources, and levels of quality are essential for your priority AI and analytics initiatives.

Where should we focus data investment?

Prioritize data-related initiatives—governance, integration, platforms—based on concrete AI and reporting needs rather than generic “modernization” goals.

How do we balance centralization and flexibility?

Decide which data capabilities should be centralized, federated, or embedded in business domains, with clear accountability models.

What data governance do we actually need?

Determine practical governance structures—roles, policies, processes—that support AI, without over-engineering bureaucracy.

How do we deal with legacy data and platforms?

Integrate or phase legacy environments in a way that keeps AI initiatives moving without assuming a full rebuild from scratch.

How do we align data with governance and risk?

Connect data policies, controls, and stewardship to the requirements of AI governance, risk management, and accountability.

Engagement formats

Typical data strategy engagement formats

Data Strategy work can range from focused assessments to comprehensive data strategy and governance design. Below are representative formats that can be adapted to your starting point and complexity.

Data landscape & readiness review

A structured review of your current data landscape against AI and analytics ambitions, identifying constraints and quick wins.

  • Inventory of key data domains and flows
  • Assessment of data quality, access, and controls
  • Readiness findings mapped to priority AI themes
Good for: early-stage clarity on “where we stand”

Data strategy & roadmap

A comprehensive engagement to define data vision, governance, architecture directions, and a multi-wave roadmap aligned with AI needs.

  • Data vision and principles aligned to AI strategy
  • Target-state data capabilities and governance outline
  • Phased roadmap linked to AI and analytics initiatives
Good for: organizations formalizing enterprise AI and data programs

Data governance & operating model alignment

A focused engagement aligning data governance, stewardship, and operating model responsibilities with AI and analytics requirements.

  • Clarified governance forums and decision pathways
  • Defined stewardship responsibilities across domains
  • Linkages into AI governance and risk processes
Good for: organizations refining roles and responsibilities
