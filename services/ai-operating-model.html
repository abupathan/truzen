Leadership questions

What we help leadership teams solve

AI success depends on clarity of roles, responsibilities, workflows, and capabilities that extend beyond technology teams. We work with executive sponsors, functional leaders, and risk and governance stakeholders to answer foundational questions about how AI will operate within the organization.

How should we structure for AI?

Define how AI-related responsibilities are distributed across business, data, technology, risk, and governance functions.

What capabilities do we need?

Identify the skills, roles, and capabilities required to build, adopt, govern, and monitor AI responsibly and effectively.

How do teams collaborate on AI?

Map collaboration patterns, hand-offs, workflows, and governance interactions across the AI lifecycle.

How do we build readiness at scale?

Develop training, coaching, and enablement programs for executives, practitioners, and business teams adopting AI.

How do we align operating model with governance?

Ensure that governance, risk, and accountability expectations are embedded directly into operating model roles and workflows.

How do we scale AI beyond pilots?

Define the operating model changes required to move from isolated experiments to repeatable, enterprise-grade AI practices.

Engagement formats

Typical operating model & readiness engagement formats

Operating model and readiness work can begin with targeted assessments or expand into comprehensive redesign across capabilities, workflows, and roles. Engagements are tailored to your AI maturity and organizational context.

Operating model baseline & readiness review

A structured review of current organizational maturity, role clarity, and workflow readiness for scaling AI adoption.

  • Stakeholder interviews & capability review
  • Assessment of collaboration & decision pathways
  • Recommended improvement areas & quick wins
Good for: early-stage or rapidly evolving AI teams

AI operating model design

A deeper engagement to design how AI is governed, developed, deployed, and monitored across the organization.

  • Role definitions & capability structure
  • Workflows, decision gates, & lifecycle integration
  • Governance & risk alignment embedded in operations
Good for: organizations ready to scale AI sustainably

AI readiness & enablement programs

Programs that equip leadership, practitioners, and business users with the knowledge and skills needed for AI success.

  • AI literacy & awareness
  • Role-specific skills development
  • Coaching, playbooks & adoption support
Good for: organizations embedding AI into everyday work
