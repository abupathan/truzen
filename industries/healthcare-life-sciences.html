Context

Why AI governance is essential in healthcare & life sciences

AI in healthcare and life sciences interacts with clinical workflows, research decision-making, operational processes, and patient or participant data. Decisions and insights must be explainable, clinically appropriate, and aligned with institutional responsibilities and applicable regulatory expectations.

Impact on patients & participants

AI can influence diagnostic support, triage, pathway suggestions, and research prioritization—requiring careful oversight and clinical judgment.

Sensitive health data

Health and research data require strong governance, access discipline, and clear understanding of appropriate vs. inappropriate use in AI systems.

Evidence & accountability

Decisions and model behavior must be documented and explainable to clinical, research, operational, and oversight stakeholders.
